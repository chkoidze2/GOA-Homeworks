Basketball


Basketball is a team sport where two teams of five players try to score points by shooting the ball into the opponents basket. The game is played on a rectangular court with two hoops at opposite ends. Basketball requires not only physical skills but also excellent coordination, strategy, and teamwork. The basic rules of basketball include each team consisting of five players, trying to score by shooting the ball into the opponent's basket. A basketball game consists of four quarters, each lasting 12 minutes. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins. Basketball is one of the most popular sports worldwide, especially in countries like the United States, China, Spain, and France. Major tournaments such as the NBA (National Basketball Association) and the EuroLeague attract millions of fans from all over the world.
